# The Complete Style Guide (100 Styles)


### 💼 Clean & Corporate
**1. Minimal:** Pure functional simplicity through negative space.
**2. Modern SaaS:** Clean, high-readability interface for digital products.
**3. Swiss:** Mathematical grid precision and bold typography.

### 🌌 Experimental & Immersive
**4. Glassmorphism:** Layered translucency with vibrant color depth.
**5. Liquid Glass:** Organic refractive distortions and mesh gradients.
**6. Aurora:** Ethereal atmospheric gradients on dark foundations.
**7. Cyberpunk:** Neon-infused technical aesthetics and dark grids.
**8. Futuristic 4D:** Immersive 3D depth and glowing spatial UI.
**9. AI Native:** Generative patterns and intelligent node-based UI.

### 🧸 Tactile & Soft
**10. Claymorphism:** Tactile, soft-shadowed 3D UI elements.
**11. 3D Illustration:** Friendly, soft-shaded characters and depth.
**12. Organic:** Earth-toned palettes and hand-drawn shapes.

### 💥 Bold & Expressive
**13. Neobrutalism:** Raw, high-contrast layouts with bold shadows.
**14. Memphis:** Eclectic geometric shapes and playful patterns.
**15. Y2K:** High-gloss chrome and retro-futuristic tech.
**16. Playful:** Bouncy micro-interactions and expressive emojis.

### 📰 Sophisticated & Editorial
**17. Luxury Editorial:** Sophisticated serif typography and muted palettes.
**18. Dark Luxury:** Refined gold accents on deep charcoal foundations.
**19. Editorial:** Narrative-driven multi-column textual layouts.
**20. Retro Futurism:** Mid-century sci-fi aesthetics and grainy textures.

### 🎛️ Modern UI & Interactions
**21. Bento Box UI:** Apple-style segmented cards and widgets with distinct border radii.
**22. Skeuomorphism:** Realistic materials, leather, metal, and lighting (classic iOS style).
**23. Neumorphism:** Soft, extruded monochromatic UI elements with inner/outer shadows.
**24. Material You (MD3):** Google's dynamic color system, pill-shaped buttons, and playful scaling.
**25. Fluent Design:** Microsoft's acrylic blur, light reveals, and deep layering.
**26. Pure Dark Mode:** OLED-friendly absolute blacks with high-contrast glowing accents.
**27. Monochrome:** A single hue with varying levels of lightness and saturation for a sleek look.
**28. Frutiger Aero:** 2000s glossy, aquatic themes with water drops, auroras, and green/blue palettes.
**29. Flat Design 2.0:** Solid colors with subtle depth, soft drop shadows, and clean vectors.
**30. Holographic UI:** Iridescent, shimmering materials and multi-color metallic gradients.

### 📼 Retro & Nostalgia
**31. 80s Synthwave:** Neon grids, outrun sunsets, chrome text, and magenta/cyan palettes.
**32. 90s Web:** Marquees, web-safe colors, chaotic layouts, and visible table borders.
**33. Vaporwave:** Pastel pinks, Roman statues, glitch effects, and Windows 95 aesthetics.
**34. Pixel Art:** 8-bit typography, nostalgic gaming aesthetics, and blocky borders.
**35. Bauhaus:** Primary colors (red, blue, yellow), functional geometry, and bold typography.
**36. Art Deco:** Gold accents, geometric luxury, and Great Gatsby-era elegance.
**37. Pop Art:** Comic book halftone patterns, bold black outlines, and vibrant solid colors.
**38. Grunge:** Distressed textures, torn paper edges, and chaotic typography.
**39. Steampunk:** Brass, gears, clockwork mechanics, and Victorian industrial elements.
**40. Typewriter/Print:** Monospaced fonts, ink bleed textures, and off-white paper backgrounds.

### 🎨 Artistic & Expressive
**41. Watercolor:** Soft bleeds, natural pigment textures, and fluid edges.
**42. Hand-Drawn:** Doodles, irregular borders, and sketch-like illustrations.
**43. Collage:** Cut-out photography, mixed media, and ransom-note text.
**44. Psychedelic:** Swirling colors, optical illusions, and warped typography.
**45. Kinetic Typography:** Moving, animated text functioning as the primary design element.
**46. Brutalist Web:** Raw, unstyled HTML default look, massive blue links, and exposed structure.
**47. Low Poly:** Faceted 3D geometry and sharp, colorful triangle meshes.
**48. Papercraft:** Origami forms, folded elements, and layered realistic drop shadows.
**49. Isometric:** Angled 3D grid perspective, miniature world feel.
**50. Line Art:** Continuous minimalist drawing, thin elegant strokes.

### 🌿 Nature & Elements
**51. Botanical:** Leaf motifs, lush greens, and floral accents.
**52. Oceanic:** Deep blues, fluid wave shapes, and underwater lighting.
**53. Earthy/Desert:** Terracotta, sand textures, and warm, dry palettes.
**54. Cosmic/Space:** Stars, nebulae, deep void backgrounds, and glowing celestial bodies.
**55. Zen/Wabi-Sabi:** Imperfect, natural, calm, with expansive negative space.
**56. Frosted Glass:** Wintery, crisp, icy translucency with cool tones.
**57. Tropical:** Vibrant jungle colors, bold flora, and high-contrast saturation.
**58. Ethereal Cloud:** Fluffy, white, airy atmosphere with soft lighting.
**59. Volcanic:** Magma glows, intense red/black contrasts, and ash textures.
**60. Biophilic:** Integrating natural organic elements directly into digital UI components.

### 🚀 Tech & Sci-Fi
**61. Glitch Art:** Chromatic aberration, data corruption, and RGB splitting.
**62. Terminal/CLI:** Green text on black background, hacker aesthetic, blinking cursors.
**63. HUD/Military:** Crosshairs, data overlays, technical readouts, and targeting brackets.
**64. Wireframe UI:** Blueprint style, technical outlines, showing the "skeleton" of the app.
**65. Outrun/Cyber-Grid:** Glowing neon floor grids converging to a vanishing point.
**66. Quantum Tech:** Abstract floating particles, glowing nodes, and connected networks.
**67. Cyber Sec:** Red/Black warning colors, secure locks, and hex dumps.
**68. Matrix Code:** Falling green characters, digital rain, and green phosphor glow.
**69. Sci-Fi Hologram:** Blue/Cyan translucent projections with scanlines.
**70. Mecha/Industrial:** Heavy metal panels, yellow warning stripes, and mechanical details.

**75. Scrapbook:** Washi tape, polaroid frames, and pinned notes.
**76. Stained Glass:** Colorful fragmented segments with heavy black leading.
**77. Arcade:** Coin-op aesthetics, pixel fonts, and bright LED dot-matrix styles.
**78. Casino:** Red velvet textures, gold trim, and flashing marquee lights.

**80. Fairy Tale:** Whimsical, storybook textures, magic sparkles, and elegant script.
