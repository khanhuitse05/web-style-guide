Questions? We've got answers.

How do I install the desktop app? expand_more

Simply click the download button at the top of the page. Once the installer downloads, open it and follow the on-screen instructions. It only takes a few seconds!

Does Spotify work on Linux? expand_more

Yes! We provide a Debian package and a Snap version for Linux users. You can find specific instructions in our support documentation.

What are the benefits of the desktop app vs the web player? expand_more

The desktop app offers offline listening, media key support, better audio quality settings, and global hotkeys for seamless control while you work in other apps.
